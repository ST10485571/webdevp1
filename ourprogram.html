Our Programs in Action

Harvest Hubs: Food Rescue Program

Our "Harvest Hubs" are the heart of our food rescue efforts. We partner with local and global supermarket chains to collect surplus, near-expiry, or aesthetically imperfect yet perfectly edible food items. This includes fresh produce, baked goods, and packaged foods that would otherwise go to waste.

Our dedicated teams ensure safe collection, rigorous quality checks, and careful sorting. The food is then packaged into nutritious meal kits or bulk supplies, ready for distribution to those in need. This program not only feeds hungry individuals but also significantly reduces food waste in South Africa.
