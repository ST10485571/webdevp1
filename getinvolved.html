Volunteer with Us

Our volunteers are the backbone of Kamvelihle Harvest Foundation. Whether you love to cook, organize, or directly engage with communities, your time and skills can make a profound impact. We need help with:

  • Preparing and packaging nutritious meals in our Harvest Hubs.
  • Sorting and organizing donated clothing and essentials.
  • Distributing food and clothing directly to communities in need.
  • Logistical support and administrative tasks.

Ready to lend a hand? Fill out the form below to express your interest!

Donate & Support Our Cause

Your generosity fuels our mission. Every contribution, big or small, directly impacts the lives of those we serve.

Monetary Donations:

Financial contributions allow us the flexibility to purchase essential items, cover operational costs, and expand our reach. As a registered Public Benefit Organisation (PBO), all monetary donations to Ubuntu Harvest Foundation are tax-deductible in South Africa (Section 18A certificates available upon request).

Food & Clothing Donations:

We gratefully accept donations of non-perishable food items, fresh produce (from commercial partners), and new or gently used clothing and blankets.

How to Donate In-Kind:

  • Drop-off at Harvest Hubs: Visit our main Harvest Hubs in Johannesburg, Cape Town, or Durban during operating hours (details in Contact section).
  • Partner Retail Stores: Look for designated Ubuntu Harvest Foundation collection bins at participating Shoprite, Pick n Pay, PEP Stores, and Woolworths branches nationwide.
  • Organize a Drive: Contact us if you'd like to organize a large-scale food or clothing drive in your community or workplace.

*Please ensure all clothing donations are clean and in good condition. For food, we prioritize non-perishable items, but commercial partners can donate surplus fresh produce directly.*
